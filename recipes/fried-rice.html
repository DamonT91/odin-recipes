Fried Rice

Description

A chinese staple, this fried rice is a sundry that will go with a multitude of different meals. Combine it with chinese, Japanese, Thai or Vietnamese food to get a real taste of the orient.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly whisked
  • 2 bacon rashers, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots, trimmed, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Shallots, sliced, extra to serve

Steps

  1. Cook the rice. Drain and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add the eggs. Swirl over base to form an ommlette. Cook until set. Transfer to chopping board.
  3. Add bacon to wok. Add shallots, peas and rice. Cook for 4 minutes, continuously stirring. Add egg and soy sauce. Top with shallots.
  4. Serve immediately
